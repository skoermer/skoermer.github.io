Biography

I am a current PhD student in the Mining and Minerals Engineering department at Virginia Tech, studying under Dr. Christopher Aaron Noble. My objective after grad school is to find an Assistant Professorship in a university that allows for a good mix of opportunities to continue applying statistical methods to mineral processing, collaborate with a group of outstanding peers, share what I have learned, and ski every now and then on the weekend.
